A B O U T M E

I am 27 years old and I live in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina. Ever since I was a child, I've been captivated by the immersive worlds created within video games. They are not just a source of entertainment for me, but a canvas for storytelling and artistic expression. My passion for crafting breathtaking environments led me to pursue a career as an environment artist. Now, I invite you to explore this space where I showcase my creations. Through my work, I aim to convey narratives, evoke emotions, and ignite a sense of wonder. Join me on this visual journey and discover more about my artistic endeavors as an environment artist!
